Exploring NYC Dept. of Corrections Inmate Data

The following is an interactive visualization of the inmates currently detained in NYC, grouped by the charges of which they are accused.

To see additional analyses of inmate demographics by charge type, please click on a single bar and scroll down. The additional info will update at the bottom of this page.

Varible Encoding:
